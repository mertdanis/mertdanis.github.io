Rules of the Game

There are 2 players and they roll a dice which has 6 numbers. Players has no limitation for their amount of roll, but if they roll 1, they are gonna lose their total dice number and automatically the turn goes for the other player. So, be careful for that. Total turn is 5, When you reach Turn 6, the game will over.
